Watercolor Wisdom

1. The "Mud" Problem

Mixing complementary colors (opposites on the wheel) creates grey or brown "mud".

Red + Green = Mud.
Blue + Orange = Mud.

Use this to your advantage to create realistic shadows instead of using Black!

2. Warm vs. Cool

Every primary color has a "bias".

Ultramarine
Cool (Towards Blue)
